[**Ring**](https://brilliant.org/wiki/ring-theory/) $(R,+,*)$ is a set $R$ defined over two operations $(+)$ and $(*)$ satisfying following ring axioms:

1. $R$ is abelian under $(+)$.
2. $R$ is closed under $(*)$ and is associative.
3. $(*)$ is distributive over $(+)$.
